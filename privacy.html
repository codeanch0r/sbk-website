Welcome to Sharples Bookkeeping ("we", "our", "us"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website www.sharplesbooks.co.uk.

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:
